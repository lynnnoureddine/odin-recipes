Creamy Banana Bread with Toppings

Description

This creamy banana bread is moist and flavorful, topped with a sweet frosting and caramelized banana slices for an extra indulgent treat. Perfect for breakfast, dessert, or a special snack.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup margarine, softened
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 ¼ cups white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup mashed bananas
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 ¼ cups all‑purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¾ cup chopped pecans
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Toppings

  • 2–3 banana slices per loaf, for caramelizing
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk or cream
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Prepare
    2.

    Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 8×4‑inch loaf pans.

  2. Create the batter
    3.

    In a large bowl, cream together the margarine and cream cheese until smooth. Gradually add the white sugar and beat until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, then stir in the mashed bananas and vanilla. Blend in the flour, baking powder, and baking soda until just combined.

  3. Mix topping
    4.

    In a small bowl, mix the chopped pecans with brown sugar and cinnamon.

  4. Assemble
    5.

    Divide half the banana batter between the prepared pans. Sprinkle the pecan mixture over top, then cover with the remaining batter.

  5. Bake
    6.

    Bake in the preheated oven for about 45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Let cool slightly before adding toppings.

  6. Caramelize bananas
    7.

    In a small skillet, melt butter and brown sugar over medium heat. Add banana slices and cook 1–2 minutes on each side until golden and caramelized.

  7. Prepare frosting
    8.

    In a small bowl, whisk together powdered sugar, milk (or cream), and vanilla until smooth.

  8. Top and serve
    9.

    Spread frosting over cooled banana bread and arrange caramelized banana slices on top. Serve and enjoy!

Tips & Tricks

  • Banana ripeness: Use very ripe bananas for the sweetest flavor and best moisture.
  • Nuts: Substitute walnuts if you prefer or omit for a nut‑free version.
  • Storage: Store leftovers tightly wrapped at room temperature for up to 3 days or freeze slices for later.
  • Toppings: Add caramelized bananas just before serving to maintain their texture and sweetness.
